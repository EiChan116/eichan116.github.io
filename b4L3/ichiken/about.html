History

1952 西区江戸堀にて河野正男が創業
1954 資本金160万円で第一建材株式会社を設立
1957 京町堀に店舗完成し移転
1970 現、第一ビルディングを京町堀に設立し事務所移転
同年、資本金を640万円に増資
1971 関西建材工事同業会を16社にて設立及び入会
1973 資本金を1280万円に増資
1979 関西建材工事同業会から全国室内工事事業会へ移行
1973 代表取締に河野毅が就任
1980 資本金を2000万円に増資
2002 河野毅が内閣総理大臣より黄綬勲章を授与
2010 代表取締役に原田雅史が就任
2022 代表取締役に豊田泰弘が就任

What we do is...

Our directors and technical team have various experiences in the construction industry, spanning around 70 years. We provide the best service with our professional team of interior walls, floors, ceilings and other details, installation, preparation and construction.
We are proud to have have been involved in big projects, working closely with the nations biggest developers in developing high rise buildings, commercial lots and residential housings.
The recent pandemic have forced many of us to be in lockdown for more than a year. This has created an interesting shift in working patterns. More and more people; both employees and employers, are more open to the idea of working from home. This led to a huge demand for home office renovation.
