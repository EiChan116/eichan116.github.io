Daiichikenzai

History

1952
Founded by Masao Kono in Edobori, Nishi Ward
1954
Established Daiichi Kenzai Co., Ltd. with capital of 1.6 million yen
1957
The store was completed and moved to Kyomachibori.
1970
Currently, the Daiichi Building is established in Kyomachibori and the office is relocated. In the same year, the capital was increased to 6.4 million yen.
1971
16 companies established and joined the Kansai Construction Materials Association
1973
Increased capital to 12.8 million yen
1979
Transitioned from the Kansai Building Material Construction Industry Association to the National Interior Construction Business Association
1973
Takeshi Kono appointed as CEO
1980
Increased capital to 20 million yen
2002
Tsuyoshi Kono awarded the Order of the Yellow Ribbon by the Prime Minister
2010
Masafumi Harada appointed as representative director
2022
Yasuhiro Toyoda appointed as Representative Director

Daiichikenzai

Daiichi Kenzai was founded in 1952. Since our founding, we have been engaged in numerous interior construction projects for offices, condominiums, stores, etc. Our role is to coordinate and consult with general contractors and workers by making full use of the experience and knowledge we have cultivated over many years.

Message Since its founding in 1954, Daiichi Kenzai Co., Ltd. has consistently made sincerity, trust and gratitude the motto of management, and has made efforts and contributed to the development of the interior decoration industry. Thanks to all of you, we will celebrate the 70th anniversary of our founding in 2026. I would like to express my deepest gratitude to all of you for your kindness and support.

We will continue to take on challenges as a group of professionals in the interior industry as we approach our 70th and even 100th anniversary. We will continue to take on new challenges by adding “professional work” and “a group that continues to take on challenges” to our existing management motto.

Please look forward to Daiichi Kenzai Co., Ltd.

Yamaguchi (Director)

It has already been 40 years since I joined the company. There were many fun and difficult times, but I am very proud to have worked at Daiichi Kenzai Co., Ltd. for 40 years. We will continue to do our best to aim for the next 50 or 55 years. Thank you in the future.

Ueda (Chief)

Half of my life has passed with Daiichi Kenzai. I believe that I was able to work for a long time because both the company and I were in good health. The company has started a new challenge towards the 70th anniversary of its founding. I will continue to challenge myself.

Masutomi (Chief)

This is my 15th year on the job. I have experienced various construction sites, but none of them are the same. It is a job that connects people, and the sense of accomplishment when the construction is completed still wells up. Our company is approaching its 70th anniversary, but I would like to continue to enjoy the sense of accomplishment for the 80th and 90th years.

Kanemaru

It's been 6 years since I joined the company. In my work, there are areas where I lack experience and knowledge, but I am pushing forward with my work with the guidance of my superiors and seniors on a daily basis. At the construction site, it is difficult to discuss and negotiate with the site supervisor and workers, but it is a job where you can feel the "sense of accomplishment" and "rewarding" when the building is completed. I would like to continue to enjoy creating spaces with dreams.
