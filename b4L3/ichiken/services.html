Our Business

For Board and Wallpaper

Plasterboard is the most commonly used base material for walls, ceilings and floors. The interior is completed by pasting wallpaper on this base. Plasterboard board is a building material with low unit price, fire prevention, and sound insulation. On top of that, put a wallpaper that mainly plays the role of aesthetics. There are a wide variety of wallpapers, and you can choose the one that matches the purpose of the space. Our role is to carefully manage the installation of boards that have this function and wallpaper that creates a dream in the space.
