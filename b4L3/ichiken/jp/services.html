Description

If you have a landed property or in a highrise, doing a major renovation is both exciting and daunting all at the same time. For example, the renovation cost of your house/building depends on several factors, including the current house condition, construction and design complexity, wet works and extensions, plumbing and electrical works, furniture and designer fees.

Luckily, ANA Build has 10 years experience in major construction projects in Malaysia. We have the expertise, supplier network, experience, and project management skills to complete the project from start to finish, within time and within budget.
